Fingers

Try to eliminate the other's hands.
Select your's and the other's hand, then the number of fingers will add toghether. If One hand gets 5 fingers it becomes eliminate. Wins the player who eliminates the other's two hands.

Tic Tac Toe

Try to align 3 images.
This is the classical game where the first player who aligns 3 of his images wins. Use your brain so the other player doesn't align his before.

Memory

Try to find the same two images.
Click on a card two know what is it. Try to find a pair of the same image by remember where were they placed. When all pairs are found, wins the player who have found more pairs.

Connect 4

Try to align 4 of your tokens.
Select one column, your token will go as down as possible. Try to align (vertically, horizontally or diagonally) 4 tokens before the other player does.
